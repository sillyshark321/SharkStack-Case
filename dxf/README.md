# Cut Files
File names are self-explanatory. The only differences are the topmost layer, where a few extra cuts are included for the OLED covers.

All files have been tested with my two Corne builds. However, since they were backed up from another CAD software, the splines are slightly messed up and the cut lines aren't joined. I will take that into consideration when making future stacked acrylic keyboard cases.

## Author's Note
Turn up your cut strength settings slightly. I used the 60W Epilog Fusion Edge 24 and a few lines on the bottom half of my acrylic sheet didn't cut all the way through.
